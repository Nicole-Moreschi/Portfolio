Design of an interactive narrative experience with the aim of raising awareness on mental illness and medication addiction. The player is assigned the role of a detective and is provided with the protagonist's diary and mobile phone: used together they allow the to user to reconnect the events and get 'into the mind' of a person who is misusing medication.
