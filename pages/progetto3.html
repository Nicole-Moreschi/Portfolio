WEBSITE BACHELOR'S THESIS

Speculative design project: the ritual of tifo (football cheering) has been purified to fit an utopian future society based on rationality and functionality.
A fictional brand (12°Uomo) has been designed to allow people to cheer (from home) through a kit and a web app. The aim of the project is to emphasize the alienation caused by the extreme pursuit of “perfection”.
12°Uomo embraces various areas of design: branding, advertising (posters and spot in motion graphics), editorial design, product design, UX/UI design and interaction design.
