PLAY

The project aims to create a virtual environment where users can interact together.
The concept is to give people the opportunity to celebrate New Year's Eve from a distance. Inside the virtual room, the pointers of all connected users are visible: they can join the choreography by clicking on the circles and sliders, which appear in time to the music. The game focuses on collaboration: if all connected users follow the instructions correctly, a visual effect reminiscent of fireworks is created.
