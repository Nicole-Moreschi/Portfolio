The project aims to celebrate the 90th anniversary of Negronetto's creation.
It's composed of three illustrations for Negroni's iconographic identity update.
The key visuals recall the 30's visual language but with a modern twist.
In detail, the first concept synesthetically evokes the sound, the second presents the famous jingle in visual form, and the third focuses on the anniversary.
